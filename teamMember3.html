Itumeleng endeavours to remain accessible to her clients and offers a well-rounded, cost effective approach to resolution of legal disputes based on her varied pool of experience.

Itumeleng has mentored up and coming attorneys throughout her career. She further cemented her commitment by becoming a member of faculty as a trial advocacy trainer who trains newly admitted attorneys in 2019. Over and above her role as an attorney, Itumeleng cares about her community and has endeavoured to give back meaningfully by being a key participant in several projects to give back to the community.

Itumeleng is a 2012 LLB graduate from the University of the Witwatersrand who got admitted as an attorney is the courts of the Republic of Botswana the same year. She is also an admitted Conveyancer and Notary Public. During her years in practice she worked at two reputable law firms in Gaborone as an associate attorney. She has explored her three offices throughout her career such that she is well experienced as an attorney focused in litigation as well as dealing with property matters in conveyancing and providing a myriad of notarial services. She started her practice as a Professional Assistant in a conveyancing department and later focused her practice on litigation which she has been focused on for over five years. Itumeleng has represented her clients in all courts of the republic of Botswana, registered Deeds, mediated disputes and drafted agreements for corporates, parastatals, banks, large and medium enterprises and individuals.
