Mr. Maswabi Mwando Maswabi, Partner
(University of Botswana)

Maswabi makes every client he interacts with feel like they are the most important client because they are very important. At the fore of Maswabi’s approach to legal practice is the ability to understand the challenges that clients and their officers deal with and what they care about, and the ability to help them successfully reach their goals.

Maswabi can be contacted on:

Maswabi is an avid litigation attorney, conveyancer and notary public with expertise and experience in debt collection, civil litigation, corporate commercial drafting and advisory, conveyancing, labour law, administration of estates and intellectual property law, to name a few areas.

Maswabi graduated with a first-class Bachelor of Laws (LLB) degree from the University of Botswana in 2011. He was admitted to practised in the same year. Maswabi has conducted numerous litigation matters in the Court of Appeal, High Court, Industrial Court, Land Tribunal and Magistrates Court with significant success. He has also served several leading banks, large and medium corporate entities, parastatals and non-governmental organisations in Botswana.

Maswabi approaches legal practice with a view of assisting clients to resolve legal matters in the most time and cost-efficient manner. To this end he keeps an eye on legal developments in Botswana and constantly looks for more efficient ways of resolving clients’ legal matters.

Maswabi’s key focus areas include:

  • Civil Litigation
  • Construction Law
  • Conveyancing and Notarial Practice
  • Contract Law and Corporate and Commercial Law
  • Debt Collection
  • Insurance Law
  • Intellectual Property Law
  • Labour and Employment Law
