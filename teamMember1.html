Mr. Eddie Ofitlhile Babuseng, Partner
(University of Cape Town)

Eddie has the natural ability to effectively communicate and connect with anyone. This ability has allowed Eddie to help clients achieve the best possible outcomes in their legal matters through negotiation, and where necessary litigation.

Eddie can be contacted on:
Tel: +267 3115000
Cell: +267 75608699
Email: eddie@babusengmaswabi.co.bw

Eddie graduated from the top law program in Africa at the University of Cape Town in 2009. He was admitted to practice as an Attorney of the High Court of the Republic of Botswana in September 2010. Post admission Eddie practiced as an Associate Attorney at a reputable local law firm and the Attorney General’s Chambers. In January 2015, Eddie joined Dow & Associates as a Partner and stayed on until founding Babuseng Attorneys at Law in April 2017. Eddie has been practicing law for over nine (9) years.

Eddie’s list of clients includes institutions who operate in an array of fields such as banking and finance, construction, insurance, milling, mining, trade unions, parastatals and government. He has extensive experience in both civil and commercial litigation.

Eddie frequently appears at the High Court, Court of Appeal, Industrial Court, Land Tribunal and in arbitration proceedings to help clients resolve their legal matters. His passion remains that of working with entrepreneurs and corporate clients. Eddie works closely with FDIs and corporate clients committed to growth, advising them on compliance and governance issues; setting out of company structures and business models; drafting of sound commercial agreements; and winding up and exit strategies.

Eddie’s key focus areas include:

  • Banking Law
  • Civil Litigation
  • Corporate and Commercial Law
  • Debt Collection
  • Insurance Law
  • Mining Law
  • Property and Land Law
  • Telecommunications Law
