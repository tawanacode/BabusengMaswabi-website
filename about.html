Our clients include banks, privately held and family-owned companies, individual business owners, entrepreneurs, insurance companies, trusts, emerging companies, manufacturers and individuals, including, but not limited to, First National Bank of Botswana Limited, Botswana Meat Commission, Bolux Group (Pty) Ltd, Fairground Holdings (Pty) Ltd, and Sunshine Insurance Company of Botswana (Pty) Ltd.

Corporate Social Responsibility

As a law firm we understand our responsibility to our community and the society at large. Therefore, we dedicate some hours to carrying out pro bono work and give out monetary donations. Most recently we have provided and continue to provide pro bono legal services to Sentebale Charity, a charity organisation registered in Botswana and headquartered in the United Kingdom. The organisation aims to transform, for the better, the lives of orphans and vulnerable children many of whom are victims of poverty and HIV/AIDS in Botswana
